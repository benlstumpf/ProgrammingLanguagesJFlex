Augsburg University is a private liberal arts university in Minneapolis Minnesota It is affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America Upon its founding in 1869 it was a Norwegian American Lutheran seminary known as Augsburg Seminarium Its first college class began in the fall of 1874 Today the university enrolls approximately 3000 undergraduate students and 800 graduate students The school is known for its emphasis on service learning volunteering in the community is both an instructional strategy and a required part of a students coursework In 2010 Augsburg was one of the six higher education institutions to receive the Presidential Award for Community Service sponsored by the Corporation for National and Community Service In 2017 the name of the school was changed from Augsburg College to Augsburg University
